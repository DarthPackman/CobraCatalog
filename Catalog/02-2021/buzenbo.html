Buzenbo - Daitengu Swordsman

Sulky Swordsman - Buzenbo is a brooding and angsty Tengu who takes himself much too seriously. He takes care to cultivate his bad-boy image by wearing a long coat and taking care of his luxuriant hair, although the top of his head is balding. Laconic and surly, he has few friends among the other Tengu, although he and Jyakubo have been friends since childhood. Jyakubo considers him his rival, much to Buzenbo’s chagrin. Buzenbo’s sword style is swift and direct. Some might believe that to be easily countered, but Buzenbo’s superior speed doesn’t leave much room for countering.
