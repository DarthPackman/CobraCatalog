Silver Rush - YuanYang Guard

Like most YuanYang he dreams of being a hero, though being a palace guard isn’t so bad. Higher ranking than a city guard, Silver Rush takes great pride in his palace issued weapons and armor, and hopes to someday join the Imperial Guard. He still has a ways to go, however; his rash nature and over-estimation of his abilities get him into sticky situations that he can’t always escape unscathed.
